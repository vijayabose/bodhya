Compose an email to a potential cloud infrastructure vendor requesting pricing information and technical details.

Context:
- Company: CloudScale Solutions
- Your company: Mid-size SaaS company with 150 employees
- Current situation: Evaluating migration from current provider
- Scale: ~500 servers, 2PB storage, high network throughput
- Decision timeline: Need to decide within 2 months
- Budget: $50K-$100K monthly range

Purpose:
- Request detailed pricing proposal
- Get technical specifications
- Understand support options
- Schedule a technical demonstration
- Begin vendor evaluation process

Requirements to ask about:
1. Pricing structure (compute, storage, network, support)
2. Available instance types and configurations
3. Storage options (SSD, HDD, object storage)
4. Network performance and bandwidth
5. High availability and disaster recovery features
6. Support levels and SLAs
7. Migration assistance services
8. Compliance certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR)

Tone: Professional, businesslike, but collaborative

Key points to include:
1. Introduce yourself and your company briefly
2. Explain why you're reaching out (evaluation process)
3. Provide context about your current scale
4. List specific information needed
5. Mention decision timeline
6. Request technical demo
7. Ask about next steps
8. Provide your contact information

Structure:
- Subject: Clear and specific
- Greeting: Professional
- Introduction: Who you are and company overview
- Current situation: Brief context
- Information needed: Organized list
- Timeline: Decision timeframe
- Next steps: Demo request
- Closing: Professional and action-oriented

Additional notes:
- Be clear about scale to get accurate pricing
- Show you're a serious potential customer
- Keep it professional and to the point (250-300 words)
- Make it easy for them to respond with the information
- Include specific technical requirements
